Plan your perfect hot springs road trip through Washington State. Select multiple springs, optimize your route, and get turn-by-turn directions.

## How to Use

1. **Select Springs**: Browse all hot springs and add the ones you want to visit
2. **Set Starting Point**: Enter your starting address or use your current location
3. **Optimize Route**: We'll calculate the most efficient driving order
4. **Explore**: View your route on the map with POI recommendations
5. **Save & Share**: Save your trip or share it with friends
