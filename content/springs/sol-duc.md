# Sol Duc Hot Springs

Olympic Hot Springs in Olympic National Park

**Temperature:** 78°F  
**Fee:** $18  
**GPS:** 47.9689° N, 123.8631° W  
**Access:** Drive-up  
**Hours:** 9 AM - 8 PM  
**Season:** Year-round  
**Clothing:** Required  

## Access & Logistics

**Road Condition:** Paved, narrow last 2 miles  
**Parking:** Lot fills by 11 AM weekends  
**Cell Coverage:** Verizon: 1 bar, AT&T: none  

## Fees & Payment

**Fee Notes:** Cash only, no ATM nearby  

## When to Visit

**Best Time:** Weekday mornings before 10 AM  
**Avoid When:** Holiday weekends  
**Maintenance:** Closed first Tuesday of each month  

## Insider Tips

The third pool from the entrance is the warmest and least crowded. Bring water shoes as the rocks can be slippery. If you're here during heavy rain, check the river level - it can flood the lower pools.

## Nearby Gems

Hike to Sol Duc Falls (1.6 miles), Appleton Pass trail for wildflowers in July, Fairy Lake for photography
